關於我們 About Us

Nexus 致力於成為校園內所有學生、社團和學校管理層之間最堅固的橋樑。我們打破年級之間的障礙，創建信息與想法自由流通的渠道。

Nexus is determined to become the strongest bridge between all students, clubs, and the school administration on campus. We break down barriers between grade levels and create a channel for the free flow of information and ideas.

學生會不僅是活動的組織者，也是學生個人和集體成長的支持者。Nexus 提供機會並搭建舞台，鼓勵每位學生探索潛能、發展領導才能並追求夢想。

The student council is not only an organizer of events but also a supporter of students' personal and collective growth. Nexus provides opportunities and builds stages, encouraging every student to explore their potential, develop leadership skills, and pursue their dreams.
