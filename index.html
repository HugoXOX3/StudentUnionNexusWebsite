關於我們 About Us

Nexus 致力於成為校園內所有學生、社團和學校管理層之間最堅固的橋樑。我們打破年級之間的障礙，創建信息與想法自由流通的渠道。

Nexus is determined to become the strongest bridge between all students, clubs, and the school administration on campus. We break down barriers between grade levels and create a channel for the free flow of information and ideas.

學生會不僅是活動的組織者，也是學生個人和集體成長的支持者。Nexus 提供機會並搭建舞台，鼓勵每位學生探索潛能、發展領導才能並追求夢想。

The student council is not only an organizer of events but also a supporter of students' personal and collective growth. Nexus provides opportunities and builds stages, encouraging every student to explore their potential, develop leadership skills, and pursue their dreams.

學生倡議 Student Advocacy

我們堅信，每一位同學的意見都值得被聆聽、被重視。Nexus作為同學與學校之間的橋樑，致力於將你的想法轉化為實際行動，共同打造更美好的校園生活。 無論是學習環境、課外活動，還是校園政策，我們鼓勵你勇敢表達，提出建議。你的倡議，是推動進步的重要力量！ We firmly believe that every student's voice deserves to be heard and valued. As a bridge between the student and the school administration, Nexus is dedicated to transforming your ideas into tangible actions, working together to create a better campus life. Whether it's about the learning environment, extracurricular activities, or school policies, we encourage you to speak up and share your suggestions. Your initiative is a vital force driving progress!

活動組織 Event Organization

Nexus致力為本校同學帶來豐富而有趣的校園生活。我們當選後，將會舉辦各種不同的活動，包括體育賽事，學術活動，以及消閒娛樂活動，讓同學在活動中享受充實的校園生活，並在不同領域有所成長 Nexus is committed to bringing a rich and engaging campus life to every student at our school. Upon our election, we will organize a diverse range of activities — including sports competitions, academic events, and recreational activities — allowing students to enjoy a fulfilling campus experience and grow across various fields.
